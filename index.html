Services

Have a look at our Services

ARCHITECTURE

Our architectural team can carry out custom and contextual designs thanks to their extensive knowledge and cutting-edge design tools. The design of office buildings, warehouses, shopping malls, markets, residential developments, airports, and bridges are just a few of the projects that WGL's architects have worked on.

CONSTRUCTION SUPERVISION

A committed group of construction supervisors, including professional engineers, experts in the built environment, technologists, and technicians, works for WGL Ltd to oversee construction. The seniority of the staff assigned to a project depends on its complexity and scale, and this decision is made in collaboration with the customer. Our own site management systems, close-out reports, and as-built drawings assist contract oversight and monitoring of construction quality.

STRUCTURING WET SERVICE

We provide services for soil and specialized chemical waste. Designing drainage systems, fire and domestic water supplies, solar water heating, and rainwater collection systems. We stay up to date on the most recent advancements in wet services technology, pushing the envelope in energy-efficient heat pumps, hot grey water harvesting for heat stripping, and recovery and re-use of grey water for irrigation.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

We offer bulk MV and LV distribution, electrical building services, solar photovoltaic and renewable energy solutions, diesel generator systems, and electrical engineering services. Our electrical engineering services include network and ICT, radio communication networks, fire detection, CCTV and access control, and building automation and management systems. Large-scale audio-visual solutions, datacenter design, pay-on-foot parking management systems, robotics and industrial automation, aviation navigation assistance

GEO-TECHNICAL RESEARCHES

Among our geotechnical services are site investigations for both NHBRC phases 1 and 2. Our investigations primarily center on building platforms, retainer wall designs, pavement layer works for highways and runways, pollution control and slime dam analysis and design, shallow foundations, and road subgrade failures. These can be used in the mining, public, and private sectors.

MUNICIPAL AND CIVIL ENGINEERING

This division serves as a support service for all sectors and is the largest division within WGL Ltd. It offers full life cycle services for civil and municipal infrastructure. These services include designing, planning, acquiring, carrying out, and managing earthworks, roads, water, and storm infrastructure for drainage and sewage, as well as water systems

BUILDING/STRUCTURAL CONSTRUCTION

Structural engineers develop and build creative responses to business difficulties using math and science. We develop a wide range of structures, including homes, office buildings, warehouses, sports arenas, mining infrastructure, and structures for the aviation industry.

PAVEMENT AND ROAD ENGINEERING

Airport runways, taxiways, and aprons (which have concrete or asphalt surfaces), as well as pavement engineering and maintenance, are all parts of the full service we offer to our clients. In terms of designing intersections, bridges, and normal road maintenance, WGL Ltd is thoroughly versed in both urban and rural road design.

INDUSTRIAL ENGINEERING

Our industrial engineering services include optimizing space utilization, process and operational flow, and 3D simulation.

TRAFFIC PLANNING

WGL LTD provides transportation a range of clients and industries are served by engineering and transportation planning services. Together with our divisions for urban infrastructure and road design, WGL Ltd offers a comprehensive, turnkey service that handles all aspects of transportation. We offer non-motorized transport studies, planning for public transit, traffic and transportation studies, traffic impact analyses, master planning for the road network, network capacity analysis, demand modeling, access management strategies, and traffic data gathering.

ENGINEERING FOR AIRPORTS

In accordance with ICAO specifications, airport engineering entails designing the airport's landside infrastructure (access roads, parking, and utilities), passenger and cargo terminals, and airside infrastructure (runways, taxiways, aprons, navigation systems, airfield ground lighting, and support of airspace design).

ECONOMIC EXAMINATION

Facilitating economic analysis is one of WGL Ltd.'s services, and as such, we have added socioeconomic studies, economic impact analyses for environmental impact assessments, economic impact assessments, and economic impact analyses to our integrated consultant service in addition to feasibility studies and business plans. Notably, WGL Ltd is dedicated to supporting regional economic growth wherever it is practical.

FEASIBILITY STUDIES

The completion of feasibility studies is a crucial step in the project's planning stage. WGL Ltd understands that carrying out feasibility studies requires an iterative process and a tiered approach. Technical soundness and economic feasibility are two factors that WGL Ltd. considers when evaluating a development's potential and use those findings to inform investment choices and funding approval. The multidisciplinary and highly skilled technical team at WGL Ltd can carry out conceptual, preliminary and final evaluations, designs, and cost estimates for projects involving the development of infrastructure and land. In order to undertake thorough evaluations, our technical team identifies important relevant institutional, technical, economic, financial, and environmental factors. This reduces uncertainty and helps to corroborate project assumptions. This service offers a platform for creating thorough business and execution strategies, as well as business motivation and substantiation for projects.

ENVIRONMENTAL ANALYSIS

WGL Ltd can offer our customers a solution that addresses several environmental domains. The following are the services that we offer to our clients and in which our environmental division has knowledge and expertise: construction environmental management plans, environmental compliance monitoring and auditing, environmental management systems, solid waste management plans, water usage permit applications, due diligence and risk assessments, strategic environmental assessments, and environmental impact assessments Plans for managing solid waste, requests for water usage permits, requests for managing specialized research, and requests for mining permits or licenses for borrow pits. To create creative, value-adding solutions, our team closely collaborates with our engineering, planning, and other experts. We take great satisfaction in applying a comprehensive approach to all of our projects, and our environmentalists have the knowledge and experience needed to carry out environmental services for a wide range of projects. In order to better serve our clients and safeguard the environment, WGL Ltd works to propose workable solutions in the event that they arise.

FINANCE/BUDGET ANALYSIS

The capabilities of WGL Ltd are utilized to create and provide information for comprehensive financial models that the client can use to assess the project's financial viability.

GEOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION SYSTEMS

SWGL Ltd has the capacity to offer our clients an extensive consulting service that addresses all facets of geographic information systems (GIS). The GIS service provides decision support systems for each project, process analysis, associated GIS technical analysis, and project development.

LEGAL

Our legal team provides support for client projects and will handle everything from securing licenses, developing legislation, regulations, and policies, to ensuring lawful operation and drafting manuals and procedures. Our services cover the tendering procedure in great detail as well. Additionally, our legal counsel offers financial, business, and contractual advice, including contract drafting, management, and negotiation. WGL Ltd can manage contractual disputes as part of this service.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Throughout the region, WGL Ltd. offers project management services to a sizable clientele of public and commercial clients. Our innovative project approach and methodology has been developed and improved, resulting in projects that meet client needs. Our strategy is founded on the tried-and-true methods and ideas found in the Project Management Body of Knowledge. We use quality, spending limits, and deadlines to guarantee the successful completion of projects. Our project management service also entails the creation of a project plan that outlines the rules, practices, and frameworks necessary to ensure efficient project management

QUANTITY SURVEYING

The Quantity Surveying Department of WGL Ltd. is made up of a group of experts with a variety of specialties and backgrounds. The mining housing projects, housing, housing, industrial, commercial, and office buildings are the key industries where WGL Ltd's quantity surveyors excel. Quantity surveyors from WGL Ltd also work on government projects like those for the construction of schools, clinics, hospitals, and various Department of Public Works projects. Economic viability analysis (feasibility studies), budget estimates and cost plans, cost planning, procurement and tender documentation, application of various contracts (such as JBCC, FIDIC, COLTO, GCC, and NEC), project coordination, costing and control of tenant and client fit-outs/installations, engineering cost management and value engineering, property development, valuations of work in progress, cash flow forecasting, and final accounting are among the services provided by the team.

VISUALISATION

For accurate depictions of how finished projects will seem, our visualisation team produces 2D and 3D artist impressions. To get the intended result, the team makes use of the most up-to-date design software and collaborates closely with our architects, town planners, engineers, and project managers. Visualizations can be used for presentations, tenders, or any marketing-related projects. They range from still renderings to animated fly-overs and walkthroughs. The team also uses cutting-edge Navisworks software to do BIM collision detection.

VIRTUAL WORLD

In addition to adding Virtual Reality (VR) to our service offerings, our visualisation team stays up to date with the most recent design trends. Through the use of virtual reality, clients can experience their projects before they are ever produced by being immersed in the environment they have imagined. Clients can not only gain a general sense of space and size by walking around a 1:1 scale model of their projects, but they can also assess functionality and accuracy before moving on with construction. WGL Ltd enables clients to experience their projects before construction begins and request adjustments that can be adopted with little or little impact on schedule and cost, whether it be one of the largest freight terminals in the world or a client's dream home.

REGIONAL AND URBAN PLANNING

The urban and regional planners at WGL Ltd. offer a wide range of services, from the various land development applications needed to obtain property development rights and/or site clearance to higher levels of planning, such as site development and master planning, precinct plans, local area plans, municipal and regional spatial development frameworks, land-use management plans and schemes, as well as urban design and development planning services. Our organization is in a good position to offer our clients a full professional service of unmatched quality thanks to our participation in several development processes. Working collaboratively with our engineers, environmentalists, architects, and other specialists, our planners have developed site evaluation procedures.
